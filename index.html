What do each letters mean?

Where you get your energy :

(E) Energized by people; talks things out to think
(I) Energized by alone time; prefers thinking quietly

How you take in information :

(S) Focuses on facts, details, and what’s real
(N) Focuses on ideas, patterns, and future possibilities

How you make decision :

(F) Makes decisions based on values and people’s emotions
(T) Makes decisions with logic and objective reasoning

How you live your life:

(J) Likes plans, structure, and early decisions
(P) Prefers spontaneity, flexibility, and keeping options open
