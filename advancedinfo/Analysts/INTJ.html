INTJ

The Architect

Architects are imaginative and strategic thinkers with a plan for everything.

Overview

Intellectually curious individuals with a deep-seated thirst for knowledge. INTJs tend to value creative ingenuity, straightforward rationality, and self-improvement. They consistently work toward enhancing intellectual abilities and are often driven by an intense desire to master any and every topic that piques their interest. INTJs don’t just learn new things for show, they genuinely enjoy expanding the limits of their knowledge. At times, INTJs may wonder whether dealing with other people is even worth the frustration.

Strengths

INTJs strive to do things their own way and prefer to take matters into their own hands. INTJs are also known for being ambitious and goal-oriented.

Weaknesses

INTJs can get impatient with anyone who seems to value feelings more than rationality and facts. INTJs relentless rationality can lead to frustration in their social lives

Relationships

Romantic: INTJs aren’t always ready to share their feelings, but it would be a mistake to think that they don’t have feelings, or that those feelings don’t run deep.

Friendships: INTJs care about depth and quality. They rather have a few good friends rather than a large circle of friends. However, even with their closest friends, INTJs may struggle to offer emotional comfort or receive it.
