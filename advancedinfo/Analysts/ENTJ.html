Overview

ENTJs are natural-born leaders. Embodying the gifts of charisma and confidence, they radiate authority in a way that draws crowds together behind a common goal. ENTJs perceive themselves to be a strong positive influence on others. ENTJs love a good challenge, big or small, and they firmly believe that they can achieve any goal with enough time and resources. To them, emotional displays are generally displays of weakness.

Strengths

ENTJs are known to be efficient, energetic, self-confident, strong-willed, strategic thinkers, charismatic and inspiring.

Weaknesses

ENTJs are stubborn and dominant, intolerant, impatient, arrogant, cold and ruthless. ENTJs are known to have poor handling of emotions.

Relationships

Romantic: Balance is key, and ENTJs should strive to meet their partner halfway, whatever their needs, be it through honest criticisms or steady emotional support and praise.

Friendship: ENTJs can sometimes come off as too impatient in friendships. However, they are usually well-intentioned, wanting only the best for their friends and for plans to run smoothly and efficiently.
