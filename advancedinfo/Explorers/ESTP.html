ESTP

The Entrepreneur

Entrepreneurs are smart, energetic and very perceptive people, who truly enjoy living on the edge.

Overview

ESTPs tend to be on the competitive side, often assuming that a competitive mindset is a necessity in order to achieve success in life. With their driven, action-oriented attitudes, they rarely waste time thinking about the past. ESTPs are a bold and brave bunch who are not afraid to take chances or act on their impulses. Sometimes ESTPs’ instantaneous observation and action is just what’s required, as in some corporate environments, and especially in emergencies.

Strengths

ESTPs’ perceptiveness isn’t used for mind games. They prefer to communicate clearly, with direct and factual questions and answers. ESTPs are full of life and energy. There is no greater joy for them than pushing boundaries and discovering and applying new tools and ideas.

Weaknesses

Living in the moment can cause ESTPs to miss the forest for the trees. They love to solves problems here and now, perhaps too much. All parts of a project can be perfect but the project will still fail if those parts do not fit together. Impatience can lead ESTPs to push into uncharted territory without thinning of the long-term consequences.

Relationships

Romantic: ESTPs’ relationships are far from boring. Their improvisational style creates a seemingly never-ending list of activities and hobbies to be involved in, and they fully encourage their partner to partake. ESTPs are adaptable, curious individuals, which certainly helps to keep the flame burning.

Friendships: ESTPs are usually the life of any social gathering. Their charismatic storytelling and exhilaration for life create a contagious energy that most find hard to resist. ESTPs are unlikely to develop friendships based solely around one-on-one discussions about global economic challenges or the role of religion in politics. Sitting idle, especially in boredom, is the bane of ESTPs’ existence.
