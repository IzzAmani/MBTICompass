INFJ

The Advocate

Advocates are Quiet and mystical, yet very inspiring and tireless idealists.

Overview

INFJs aren’t content to coast through life, they want to stand up and make a change. For them, success doesn’t come from money or status but from seeking fulfillment, helping others, and being a force for good in the world. They care about integrity and they are rarely satisfied until they’ve done what they know to be right. They move through life with a clear sense of their values and they aim to never lose sight of what truly matters, not according to people or society at large but according to their own wisdom and intuition. INFJs value deep, authentic relationships with others. They enjoy meaningful conversations far more than small talk. For INFJs, one of the most rewarding aspects of life is seeking out a purpose. And once they’ve found it, they strive to do it justice.

Strengths

INFJs crave a sense of purpose in life. They are energised and impassioned by the beauty of their visions of the future. INFJs embrace their creative side and are always on the lookout for opportunities to express themselves and think outside the box.

Weaknesses

INFJs are often sensitive to criticism, they can become defensive, dismissive or angry. INFJs are prone to burnout due to their perfectionism traits, which leads to exhausting themselves if they don’t balance their drive to help others with necessary self-care and adequate rest.

Relationships

Romantic: INFJs are warm, caring, honest and insightful. They patiently work to uncover their partner’s innermost needs and desires. One of the things INFJs find most important is establishing genuine, deep connections with the people they care about.

Friendships: INFJs aren’t just searching for someone to spend time with. They want to find someone who resonates with their passions and their convictions. Once they find genuine friendships, INFJs make loyal and caring companions. At times they may even be surprised by the fierceness of the loyalty and devotion that they feel toward their friends.
